MATA SUNDRI COLLEGE FOR WOMEN

BSc. (Hons) Computer Science

Department of Computer Science



Eligibility

Only female candidates; must have passed(10+2) with Maths as compulsory subject. Selection would be done on the basis of CUET scores with Maths and English as compulsory subject

Fees

The fees would be dependent on guidelines given by DU for the academic year. For academic year 2022-2023 , first year UG fees was Rs.27500
